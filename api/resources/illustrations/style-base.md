# Illustration style guide

This file is the shared style prompt for every AI-generated illustration. The
`Illustrator` service collapses it into a single paragraph and prepends it to
every request, together with the character prompt (`character-<member>.md`)
and style reference sheet (`<member>-style-reference.png`) of each family
member in the scene, plus the chroma-key background paragraph
(`background-chroma.md`). Keep everything below written as generation-ready
prose.

Friendly hand-drawn illustration in the family's sketchbook style: loose,
slightly irregular black ink line art drawn by hand, flat solid fills, no
shadows, no gradients, no textures, no 3D, no realism, no text or letters.
Faces and skin are always opaque paper white, never colored. Each family
member keeps their own outfit and garment color from their reference sheet;
beyond that, use only a few soft colors per scene that harmonize together.
The tone is always gentle, didactic and kind — the scene must be instantly
recognizable to a 4-6 year old, and never aggressive, scary or shaming, even
for difficult behaviors. Composition: one clear subject centered with
generous empty space around it, at most one or two extremely simple props,
no scenery, no visual clutter. Maximum simplicity: fewer details, more
intention.
